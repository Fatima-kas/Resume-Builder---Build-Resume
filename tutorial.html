How to Use the Resume Builder

- Follow this guide to create and download your professional resume.

Step 1: Enter Your Details

Fill in your name, contact information, and a short "About Me" section. Ensure everything is accurate.

Step 2: Choose a Template

Select from multiple templates to suit your style—modern, minimalistic, or bold.

Step 3: Add Skills, Education & Experience

Highlight your skills and list your educational qualifications and work experience clearly.

Step 4: Preview & Download

Use the preview feature to ensure formatting looks great. Click download to save your resume as a PDF.
